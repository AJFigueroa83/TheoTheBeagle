Hello! My name is Theo.

BIO

Theo was born on November 20, 2020 in Missouri.

He is the runt of his litter and had some mild complications during birth. He was born with a soft spot on his head which meant he had to be carefully watched so that he wouldn't get hit in that area.

His breeders made sure to take very good care of him. Infact, when my wife and I wanted to give Theo (then called Mickey Mouse) a permanent loving home, the breeder said they would not part ways with him until they were sure that he was 100% healthy.

So, after waiting for about 8 weeks we were finally given the clearance to take Theo home with us and he has been a part of our family since January 29, 2021 in Savannah, Georgia. Here you can learn more of his day to day life as a member of the Figueroa family.
